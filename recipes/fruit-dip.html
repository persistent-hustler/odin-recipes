Fruit Dip

Description

This fruit dip is really light and fluffy. Great to serve with your favorite fruit at parties, holidays, or any time!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 jar marshmallow creme

Steps

  1. Gather cream cheese and marshmallow creme.
  2. Place cream cheese and creme in a medium bowl
  3. Blend cream cheese and marshmallow creme with an electric mixer until smooth and well combined.
  4. Serve with fruit and enjoy!
